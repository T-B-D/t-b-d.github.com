    TPB AFK: The Pirate Bay Away From Keyboard

    Help documentary filmmaker Simon Klose develop "The Augumentary" - a way to remotely control embedded links in the upcoming film TPB AFK: The Pirate Bay Away From Keyboard. By incorporating links into the documentary using html5 we will empower the audience with information related to the film. By remotely controling the links via a smartphone or tablet, the audience will get the links synched to the scene they're watching while still in the sofa. Help us develop a revolutionary remote control app!

    http://www.tpbafk.tv/2012/01/are-we-first-on-earth-with-an-html5-remote-tpb-afk-augumentary-v-0-2-ftw/

    Inuse

    Book the room

    We would like to have a solution for booking conference rooms. The idea is that when you enter the room, it will show on a screen if the room is available or if someone has already booked it. If it is free you should be able to make a gesture (thumbs up) and book it for the next hour.

    1. 1.In the room there is a Kinect, a computer and a screen.
    2. 2.Use Kinect to recognize when someone enters the empty room and make it possible to book the room with a simple gesture.
    3. 3.Confirm on the screen that the room is booked for an hour.
    4. 4.When the room is empty again the booking will be erased.

    White board documentation

    We would like a solution where everything that is written on the white board in our conference room gets saved.

    1. 1.Use Kinect to document the growing writing on the white board.
    2. 2.Document in a suitable format/way so it is possible to go back and follow the growth. The idea is not to record everything but to capture when something interesting happens (think key events or one picture per second). Think about the possibility to combine the recording with sound from the session.
    3. 3.The session should be tagged and saved for later reference.

    Bounty: iPad 2

    http://inuse.se/
