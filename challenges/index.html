    FlatFrog

    Based on a unique patented optical in-glass solution FlatFrog introduces this spring the Multitouch 3200 – a 32” multi touch table with supreme performance. Now we want to challenge you to build great applications for the exciting new field of multi-user multi-touch tables. Receive touch data via standard API:s in Windows (over USB) or receive them as UDP packets over TUIO (http://www.tuio.org/). What would you like to do with your friends with a touch enabled coffee table while sipping your espresso? For more info visit
